Patient Voices & Success Stories

Hear firsthand from patients and see the impact of our work through a collection of stories and testimonials.

Krishna, Mamidipally, Hyderabad

"After my parents learned my blood group did not match theirs, we were worried. Cheyutha Foundation helped me register for the JEEVANDAAN Scheme, giving me a place in the queue for a new kidney and new hope for my future."
— Krishna, Kidney Transplant Candidate

Pension for Kidney Transplant Recipients in Andhra Pradesh

"Through our collective effort and persistent calls to the 'Dial your Arogya Sri CEO' program, the Cheyutha Foundation was able to advocate for and secure a monthly pension of Rs. 5000 for kidney, liver, and heart transplant patients across the state."
— A Community Success Story

Our Founder Recognized as a Times Hyderabad Hero

"The Times of India nominated our founder, Mr. Bhagavan Reddy, as a 'Times Hyderabad Hero' on March 4, 2020, in recognition of his tireless efforts in helping kidney patients and raising awareness about organ donation."
— Cheyutha Foundation

"My wife gave me a second chance to Live"

"The cost of my transplant was an unbearable burden. But the founders of Cheyutha Foundation counseled my family and with their help, the cost was reduced from Rs. 12 lakhs to Rs. 6 lakhs. Today, I am healthy and living a normal life with my family."
— Mr. Srinivasa Rao, Kidney Transplant Recipient

Cheyutha Foundation Recognized at Dr. M. Channa Reddy's Centenary Celebrations

"The Cheyutha Foundation was honored and recognized for its work at the Birth Centenary Celebrations of Dr. M. Channa Reddy at Shanti Sarovar, demonstrating the impact of our mission on a wider scale."
— Cheyutha Foundation

"My mother gave me a second chance to Live"

"Thanks to the foundation's motivation and guidance, I was able to receive a kidney from my mother. The foundation also helped reduce my treatment costs by a significant amount and now provides me with a monthly pension."
— Mr. Swamy, Kidney Transplant Recipient
