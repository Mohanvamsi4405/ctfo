"Free Nutrition Guides in English"

The provided document, "Nutritional Secrets," is a guide for chronic kidney disease (CKD) patients in India. It was developed by the Kidney Warriors Foundation with support from numerous hospitals and nutrition experts. The book offers diet plans, recipes, and tips for managing CKD, dialysis, and post-transplant health.
