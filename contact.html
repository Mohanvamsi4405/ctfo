Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mission, services, and how you can help.

Cheyutha Foundation for Organ Transplants (CFOT) is a social organization founded in 2015 by renal transplant recipients. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive support to patients with kidney diseases, including those on dialysis or awaiting transplantation, across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

There are many ways to get involved! You can make a donation, volunteer your time and skills, or help spread awareness about organ donation. Visit our "Get Involved" page for more details on how you can make a difference.

We work to provide financial support and emotional assistance to needy patients wherever possible. Our goal is to alleviate the burden of healthcare costs and ensure patients have access to the care they need. We also help patients access government schemes and other support programs.

Our office is located at 4-211/159/A/1, Northgate Avenue, Opp. Infant Jesus International school, Shamshabad, Rangareddy district -501218. You can find our updated contact information and a map on this page.

We offer educational resources on new treatments, post-transplant care, and lifestyle management for patients. We organize regular sessions with doctors and medical professionals to keep our community informed and empowered.
