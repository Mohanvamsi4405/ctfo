Learn More About Us
The first social organization formed by Renal Transplant Recipients, supporting kidney patients across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh with comprehensive kidney care, education, and community building since 2015.

Our Impact in Action

Since 2015, Cheyutha Foundation has been dedicated to empowering lives through comprehensive kidney care support. This video highlights our journey, showcasing the resilience of our patients and the vital role of our community in providing emotional and financial support. Your contribution helps us provide critical care, educational resources, and a supportive network for those undergoing dialysis or transplantation.

Our Programs

Education on New Treatments

Educating members about the latest treatments in kidney care and organ transplantation.

Support for Needy Patients

Providing financial and emotional support to patients in need.

Subsidized Medicines & Kits

Offering medicines at subsidized costs and supplying dialyzer kits for dialysis patients.

Regular Health Check-ups

Assisting patients with regular health check-ups and monitoring.
